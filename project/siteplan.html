Website Plan

ELIZGATH COFFEE

Site Name

ELIZGATH COFFEE

This name represents a premium coffee brand that combines traditional brewing methods with modern flavors. "Elizgath" is derived from "Elizabeth" (representing tradition) and "Gath" (representing strength and foundation).

Optional domain availability: elizgathcoffee.com (available)

Site Purpose

The site will provide:

  • Showcase our premium coffee blends and single-origin offerings
  • Online ordering system for coffee beans and merchandise
  • Information about our coffee brewing techniques and recipes
  • Location and hours for our physical coffee shops
  • Subscription service for regular coffee deliveries

Scenarios

Target audience questions:

  1. What coffee blend would you recommend for someone who prefers a smooth, low-acidity taste?
  2. Do you offer any seasonal or limited edition coffee flavors?
  3. How can I set up a recurring delivery of my favorite coffee blend?
  4. What are the differences between your light, medium, and dark roast options?
