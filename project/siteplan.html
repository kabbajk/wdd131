LizaGath Coffee Shop

Website Planning Document

Site Name

LizaGath Coffee Shop

Domain Availability: lizagathcoffee.com

Rationale: The name "LizaGath" combines the coffee brewing process with the idea of a welcoming space. It's memorable, easy to spell, and clearly communicates our coffee shop focus.

Site Purpose

The LizaGath website will serve as the digital hub for our artisan coffee shop, providing:

  • Information about our specialty coffee blends and brewing methods
  • Details about upcoming events and workshops
  • Our story and commitment to sustainable coffee sourcing
  • Contact information and location details
  • Online contact form for customer inquiries

The site will establish our brand identity and serve as a marketing tool to attract new customers while keeping regulars informed.

Scenarios

Our target audience includes coffee enthusiasts, local professionals, and students. Key scenarios include:

  1. "What coffee blends do you currently feature, and what are their flavor profiles?" - Answered by our featured blends section with interactive flavor map.
  2. "When is your next latte art workshop, and how can I sign up?" - Answered by our events calendar with RSVP functionality.
  3. "What are your hours and location?" - Clearly displayed in the footer and contact page.
  4. "What makes your coffee shop unique?" - Answered in our "Our Story" section highlighting our sustainable sourcing and artisan approach.
